About Our Community

RUNWITHBOCA brings together runners of all levels in South Florida. We believe in the power of community, the beauty of consistency, and the joy found in every mile shared together.

What We Offer

  • Group Runs Weekly morning and evening sessions for all paces
  • Training Programs Structured coaching for marathon prep and 5K goals
  • Community Events Social gatherings and race day team support
  • Mentorship Experienced runners guiding newcomers to success

Event Guidelines & Requirements

To ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for everyone, please adhere to the following guidelines and bring the required items.

  • Age: Participants must be between 18 and 50 years old.
  • Footwear: A pair of running shoes and a separate pair of normal shoes for after the run.
  • Apparel: Extra socks, a fresh shirt, shorts, and a towel.
  • Accessories: A running bag to hold your personal items.
  • Safety: For your safety and to prevent loss, please do not wear necklaces or loose jewelry during runs.
